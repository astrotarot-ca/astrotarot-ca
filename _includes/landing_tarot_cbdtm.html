CBD Tarot de Marseille Deck

From the french school of esoterism, this 400 year old and some deck is inspired by an even older deck of cards — the arabic Mameluk era card game. Composed of a major arcana of 22 cards and a minor arcana with 4 suites, the magical 78 card CBD Tarot de Marseille game has inspired many generations across many social classes for gentrification.

Originally printed by Nicolas de Conver of Marseille in the 14th century, the images used in this game are the ones meticulously reproduced by Dr. Yoav Ben-Dov and given to the online community for usage by anyone who is interested to personaly use them as a study tarot deck.
