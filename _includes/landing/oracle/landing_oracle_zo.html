Zodiac Oracle

This game of oracle has foremost been created to study Astrology and I hope astrology students will be tempted to use it also. With 10 cards for the Planets, 12 cards for Constellations, and 12 cards for the Houses, the three card game is quite simple—pick one from each pile and have your revelation, or pull as many as you want.

For those unfamiliar with astrology it is still a very simple game. Pull one planet card, one constallation card, and one house card, and along the keywords of each card you'll have parts of a sentence that you be able to decipher.
